Recording Artist Betusile Mcinga
BIO
The Story

Born in Willowvale and raised in Mthatha, Betusile Mcinga is a true son of the Eastern Cape who was shaped by the iconic musical legacy of his mother, the multi-award-winning Gospel artist Lusanda Mcinga. From his early days as a keyboardist and backing vocalist in the Lusanda Spiritual Group to becoming a multi-award-winning solo powerhouse himself, his journey is defined by both grace and excellence

With platinum-selling hits like 'Ndiyanqophisa' and 'Kuphelele', Betusile has evolved into an independent industry leader. Beyond the music, his heart remains with the community; constantly giving back to the schools and orphans of his home province through his foundation.
